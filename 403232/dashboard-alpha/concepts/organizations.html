Organizations

What is an organization?

An organization is a pool of Dashboard users that belong to one or more teams under an organization owner.

View your organizations

Dashboard users can belong to multiple organizations at once. Select the Organizations option from the user menu in the top right corner of the page to view the list of organizations that you belong to.

The default organization

All Dashboard accounts come with their own organization. The original name of the organization includes its owner’s public name: [public name] Organization. You can rename your organization at any time.

Initially, the owner of the account is that organization’s only member. You cannot create additional organizations, but you can invite other users to join your organization.

Add users to your organization

To add new users to your organization, invite them to one of the teams in your organization.

Join an organization

To join an existing organization, you need to join a team that belongs to the organization. Ask the owner of the team you want to join to send you an invitation link.

Switch between organizations

Members of multiple organizations can access the Organization sidebar menu. The sidebar displays projects and teams that are related to the active menu entry.

Leave an organization

Open the Organizations page and click the Leave button next to the organization entry to leave this organization. Organization owners cannot leave their own organizations.

Organization management

Organization owners can rename the organization, and create teams within the organization.

Rename an organization

To edit the name of an organization, open the Organizations page and click the name of an organization that you own. Click away or press Enter to save the new name.
